Becoming a pharmacy technician is a great opportunity to enter the pharmaceutical field and decide what you will do with your career on the long run. It has potential for growth and can be a good seasonal job for our youth to earn decent wages and familiarize themselves with pharmacy. It doesn’t ask for so many requirements:

  1. start by getting a job as a typist with one the major chain pharmacies (the hiring rate is increasing ) and earn hours of practice of at least 400 hours
  2. start preparing for the PTCB examination. Visit this link for more information and don’t forget to create a profile
  3. for foreign graduates get a notarized copy of your foreign secondary school diploma it will be needed to apply for PTCB and with the board of pharmacy
  4. after passing the PTCB exam, send the required documents to the board of pharmacy and follow the steps in this form

    5. - Once you’re a licensed pharmacy technician the true journey begins by either working as the title job or growing into your role by becoming:

    • A senior pharmacy technician
    • A pharmacy store manager
    • A pharmacy scheduler
